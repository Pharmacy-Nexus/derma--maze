# Derma-Maze V5 Brand System

## Main palette
- Navy 950: `#071126`
- Navy 900: `#0B1730`
- Navy 800: `#13264A`
- Gold 500: `#C7A35A`
- Gold 400: `#D9BD82`
- Cream 100: `#F3EFE7`
- Paper: `#FFFDF8`

## Rules
- Navy is the dominant brand color for navigation, hero sections, footers, overlays, and loaders.
- Gold is reserved for primary calls to action, active states, key statistics, and focus details.
- Chapter pages now inherit the same navy/gold identity.
- Updates and Shipping & Returns are removed from navigation, footer, and sitemap and are marked `noindex`.
- The printed book is the primary product. The website is a companion platform; no PDF edition is offered.
- The maze loader appears once per session at full duration and briefly on later page loads.
