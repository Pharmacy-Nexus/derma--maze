الهدف من المشروع هو تحويل موضوعات الأمراض الجلدية المعدية من معلومات متفرقة إلى مسار واضح: التعرف على العلامة، تضييق التشخيصات المحتملة، اكتشاف علامات الخطر، ثم فهم منطق اختيار العلاج.

AUTHOR & PROJECT

Eslam Nasser

Author of Derma-Maze — Volume 1, an educational project presenting dermatology pharmacotherapy through a visual, structured approach that connects clinical appearance, reasoning, and treatment selection.

The Derma-Maze idea

The project turns infectious skin-disease topics into a clear pathway: recognize the sign, narrow the differential, detect red flags, and understand the logic behind treatment selection.

Project principles

  • Topic-based organization rather than disconnected memorization.
  • Connecting knowledge with cases, treatment questions, and images.
  • A bilingual experience for pharmacists and pharmacy students.
  • A clear boundary between general education and individual medical assessment.

The book and the website

The website is a companion to the book, not a complete digital copy. It provides chapter maps, question banks, and limited previews, while the full explanations, tables, algorithms, and content remain in the printed book.
